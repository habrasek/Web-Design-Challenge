Cloudiness and Latitude

Similar to humidity and latitude, there is an extremely weak correlation between latitude and cloudiness. Clouds are what rain falls from and the rainier places are normally more cloudy. How rainy a place is gets determined by which biome it is located in. The Atacama Desert is the dryest on the planet and gets little cloud cover or rain. If you stay on the same latitude and go east towards Brazil, there are lots of rain forests which have lots of cloud cover.
