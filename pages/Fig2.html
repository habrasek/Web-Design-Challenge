Humidity and Latitude

Unlike temperature, humidity is impacted by the biome of a region as opposed to the level of sun exposure. For instance, Miami and Abu Dhabi are both very hot places and their latitudes are pretty similar, but the humidity levels are very different. This is because Miami is more tropical and Abu Dhabi is a desert. As a result, there is not a strong correlation between humidity and latitude.
