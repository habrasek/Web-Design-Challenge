Maximum Temperature and Latitude

A latitude of 0 degrees represents the border between the northern and southern hemisphere, also known as the euqator. Because of this, the cities on the equator get more sunlight and thus higher temperatures. This is why the maximum temperatures get lower the further one is from the equator.
