Wind Speed and Latitude

Similar to humidity and latitude as well as cloudiness and latitude, there is an incredibly weak correlation between wind speed and latitude. There are a lot of factors that go into determining how windy a place is. One interesting thing is that the highest windspeeds are found in the Northern Hemisphere. This could be due to the jetstream as well as Hurricane seasons.
